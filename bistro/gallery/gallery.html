Not only is our food good, it's also good-looking! Our patrons often stop to admire our fare with a quick Instagram snap before digging in. We've collected a few of our favorite shots here.

Our Baked Goods


We start our day at the crack of dawn to bake our own muffins, bread, and dinner rolls. Loaves not used that day are donated to the local food shelter.

Our Burgers


People come from all over to enjoy our lovingly made burgers. We grind our own locally-sourced organic beef and turkey so you know it's fresh and free from fillers and other nonsense. Go for one of our creative topping combos or stick with the classics.

Catch of the Day


Our chef works with local fisherman to pick the freshest the sea has to offer for our daily seafood special. Our Roast Cod Caponata with Roasted Potatoes is an old favorite with our regulars.

Our Housemade Fries


Can't complete your meal without a side of our seasoned in house fries. Baked or fried at your choice and cut just the way our regulars like it.

Finger-Licking Good


Our fried chicken has a crisp, flavorful outside with a tender and juicy inside. Stop by to grab a batch of our fried chicken wings.

Fresh and Flavorful


Looking for a light yet tasty side or meal? Try our tabouleh salad, made with hand-picked ingredients from our local garden so you don't miss out on the fresh flavors.
